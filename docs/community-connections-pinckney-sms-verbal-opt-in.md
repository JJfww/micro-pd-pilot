# Community Connections at Pinckney SMS Verbal Opt-In Language

Community Connections at Pinckney is a school-based program operated within Unified School District 497 in Lawrence, Kansas.

Staff participants are asked the following during an in-person professional development meeting:

> "Community Connections at Pinckney is offering a pilot SMS update program related to this initiative. If you agree to participate, you may receive recurring text messages about reminders, updates, and follow-up resources. Message frequency will vary based on the program schedule. Message and data rates may apply. Participation is voluntary, and you may reply STOP at any time to opt out or HELP for assistance. Do you agree to receive these text messages?"

Only staff participants who verbally agree are enrolled in the program.

After opt-in, staff participants receive an initial confirmation text message:

> "You are enrolled in Community Connections at Pinckney text updates. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out."
