# Community Connections at Pinckney SMS Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

## Program Name

Community Connections at Pinckney Text Updates

## Program Description

Staff participants who opt in will receive SMS messages related to Community Connections at Pinckney, a school-based program operated at Pinckney Elementary School within Unified School District 497 in Lawrence, Kansas. Messages may include reminders, schedule updates, participation-related notices, and follow-up resources.

## Opt-In

Staff participants opt in verbally during an in-person staff professional development meeting. Only staff participants who directly consent to receive text messages are enrolled.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the program schedule.

## Cost

Message and data rates may apply.

## Opt-Out

Staff participants may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

## Help

Staff participants may reply HELP for assistance.

## Support Contact

For support, contact [district or school contact email] or [district or school phone number].
