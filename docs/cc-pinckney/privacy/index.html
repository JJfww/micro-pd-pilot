Community Connections at Pinckney SMS Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Community Connections at Pinckney is a school-based program operated within Unified School District 497 in Lawrence, Kansas.

For this SMS professional development program, Unified School District 497 may collect staff participants' names, mobile phone numbers, and limited participation information for the purpose of administering PD to Community Connections at Pinckney staff. This information is used only to send program-related text messages, including reminders, updates, and follow-up resources.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except with service providers that support message delivery for this program.

Participation in this program is voluntary. Staff participants may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, reply HELP.

For questions about this privacy policy or this SMS program, contact Jenna Viscomi, Principal, or Jennifer Burnes, Transition Specialist and Technical Administrator for this program, at Community Connections at Pinckney at 785-852-5800 or jennifer.burnes@usd497.org.
